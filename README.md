## Tools

### 1. [Trading Fee Calculator](https://marklulu123.github.io/stock-trading-tools/fee-calculator.html)

Backtest dollar-cost averaging into Tesla, Nvidia, Apple, S&P 500, and more. Compare DCA vs lump sum investing with visual charts.

## Why These Tools?

Millions of international investors want to buy US stocks like Tesla and Nvidia, but:
- Traditional US brokers require SSN and US bank accounts
- Fee structures are confusing and hide real costs
- It's hard to compare platforms objectively

These tools help you make informed decisions based on your specific situation.
